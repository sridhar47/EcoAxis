about teraspin | ate facility

Our vision is to be a “value driven, world class group, spearheading innovative and sustainable solutions”. Working towards this vision, A.T.E. has developed 5 core technologies – textile spinning, M2M, wastewater treatment, heat exchange, static and ink control. We have several patents to our credit. Our core technologies are developed through our deep domain expertise in the fields, assimilation of customer insights, dedicated research and development, as well as our commitment to addresses customers’ business and technology demands sustainably. Manufactures precision spinning machinery components comprising drafting systems, spindles, and other precision spinning machinery components and accessories under the brand TeraSpin. Designs and manufactures indirect and direct evaporative cooling products with the innovative heat exchanger technology – DAMA (Dry Air Moist Air) – at their core. Also manufactures air-to-air heat exchangers that conserve energy and provides operational savings. The IoT-based continuous remote monitoring system enables effective and efficient management of industrial plants and equipment. Already productised for various segments, this technology allows for the seamless integration of data acquisition, M2M communications into a real or near real-time database, Big Data analytics, and industrial process know how. Designs and manufactures static control and ink handling and conditioning systems that are designed to improve safety, productivity, and quality for a plethora of industries. Introduced several innovations in the field of industrial wastewater treatment such as AAA (anerobic, aerobic, air flotation), AVR (Anerobic Venturi Reactor), AHR (Anaerobic Hybric Reactor) and the like.
